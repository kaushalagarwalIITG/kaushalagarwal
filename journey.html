Research Journey

My research journey has evolved from classical plant sciences to interdisciplinary research at the interface of plant biology, microfluidics, mechanics, nanotechnology, and computational modelling. My goal is to develop innovative technologies that improve our understanding of plant-environment interactions and contribute to sustainable agriculture.

2013

First Research Experience

Conducted an independent study on estimating plant pigments using paper chromatography during higher secondary education. This was my first exposure to experimental plant science.

2015

JBNSTS Scholar

Received the Senior Encouragement Award from the Jagdish Bose National Science Talent Search (JBNSTS), recognizing academic excellence and scientific potential.

2015–2018

Bachelor of Science (Botany)

Completed B.Sc. in Botany from Siliguri College with First Rank. Developed a strong foundation in plant taxonomy, anatomy, physiology, ecology, biodiversity, and microscopy.

2018–2020

Digital Plant Biodiversity Project

Led the geo-tagging and digitalization of shrubs and trees at Ramakrishna Ashram, creating a digital taxonomic database accessible through geospatial documentation. Presented the work at regional and national scientific conferences.

2019–2020

Master's Research

Completed M.Sc. in Botany from the University of North Bengal with Second Rank. Research focused on metallic nanoparticles and nano-conjugates for controlling fungal pathogens while improving plant growth and physiological responses.

2019–2020

Research Awards

Received the Outstanding Paper Award at the 4th Regional Science and Technology Congress (2019) and the National Seminar at the University of North Bengal (2020).

2020

Competitive Achievement

Qualified the Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) in Life Sciences with AIR 214.

2022–2023

Junior Research Fellow

Worked at IIT Guwahati on the project "Establishing Efficient Platform for Genetic Engineering in Indian Tea", gaining experience in plant biotechnology, tissue culture, and genetic engineering.

2023

Entrepreneur in Residence

Joined the Technology Innovation Hub (TIH), IIT Guwahati as an Entrepreneur in Residence, contributing to interdisciplinary technology development and innovation.

2023–Present

Doctoral Research

Started Ph.D. at IIT Guwahati under the supervision of Dr. Pranab Kumar Mondal. Current research focuses on phytofluidics, plant biomechanics, transport phenomena, root microenvironment engineering, and plant stress biology.

2023

First International Conference in Phytofluidics

Presented "Flow-induced Thigmomorphogenesis of Plant Root: The Role of Microfluidics" at the 1st Indian Conference on Micro Nano Fluidics (ICOM 2023), IIT Madras.

2024

Lab on a Chip Cover Article

Published "Unveiling Nutrient Flow-Mediated Stress in Plant Roots Using an On-chip Phytofluidic Device" in Lab on a Chip. The article was selected as a Cover Article and received nationwide media coverage.

2024

Expanding Phytofluidics

Contributed to research on intermittent flow in plant roots and paper-based lateral flow diagnostics through publications in Physics of Fluids.

2025

Langmuir Cover Article

Published "Bringing Field to the Lab: An In Silico Analysis of Oxygen Production in Aquatic Plants" in Langmuir. The work was selected as a Cover Article.

2025

Journal of Fluid Mechanics

Contributed to research published in the Journal of Fluid Mechanics on transport phenomena in microchannels and assisted in the preparation of the graphical abstract and scientific illustrations.

2025

Patent

Filed the patent "QUALITEA: A Paper-Based Colorimetric Test Strip for Assessing Tea Purity and Quality", introducing a rapid paper-based analytical device for tea quality assessment.

2025

International Conference

Presented "Gravitropic Conversations in Plants: Tracking Root Dynamics and Response to Gravity Using Phytofluidics" at the International Conference on Micro Nano Fluidics (ICOM 2025), IIT Guwahati.

2026

Soil Matrix-on-a-Chip (SMaC)

Developed the Soil Matrix-on-a-Chip (SMaC) platform to investigate root morphodynamics, biomechanics, and root-soil interactions in structured soil environments.

2026

Best Oral Presentation

Received First Prize in Oral Presentation at the 4th Botanical Congress for presenting research on root morphodynamics in Soil-on-a-Chip systems.

2026

Review Article

Published the review article "Microfluidics in Plant Science: Emerging Technologies and Applications", summarizing recent advances in phytofluidics and plant microfluidic technologies.

Current Research

Interdisciplinary Research

Current work integrates phytofluidics, plant biomechanics, microfluidics, computational modelling, advanced imaging, Raman spectroscopy, nanobiotechnology, biosensors, and sustainable agriculture to understand plant behaviour across multiple scales.

Future Vision

Establishing Phytofluidics

My long-term vision is to establish Phytofluidics as a globally recognized interdisciplinary research field by combining plant biology, engineering, fluid mechanics, artificial intelligence, and advanced sensing technologies to address future agricultural challenges.
