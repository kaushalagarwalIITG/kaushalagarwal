Awards & Honors

Throughout my academic journey, I have received recognition for research excellence, scientific publications, conference presentations, and academic achievements. These recognitions reflect my commitment to interdisciplinary research in plant biology, microfluidics, and sustainable agriculture.

🏆 Best Oral Presentation Award

First Prize at the 4th Botanical Congress (2026) for presenting research on Root Morphodynamics in Soil Matrix-on-a-Chip (SMaC).

🌟 Cover Article

Lab on a Chip (2024)
"Unveiling Nutrient Flow-Mediated Stress in Plant Roots Using an On-Chip Phytofluidic Device" was selected as a Cover Article.

🌟 Cover Article

Langmuir (2025)
"Bringing Field to the Lab: An In Silico Analysis of Oxygen Production in Aquatic Plants" was selected as a Cover Article.

📰 Research Featured in Media

The Lab on a Chip publication received national and institutional media coverage for its contribution to plant microfluidics and phytofluidics.

💡 Patent

Filed a patent on QUALITEA, a paper-based colorimetric device for rapid assessment of tea purity and quality.

🌍 International Conferences

Presented research at the International Conference on Micro Nano Fluidics (ICOM 2023 and ICOM 2025), showcasing advances in phytofluidics and plant biomechanics.

🏅 Scientific Publications

Published research in leading international journals including Lab on a Chip, Langmuir, Physics of Fluids, Journal of Fluid Mechanics, and other peer-reviewed journals.

🤝 Research Leadership

Contributed to establishing Phytofluidics as an interdisciplinary research area integrating plant biology, microfluidics, biomechanics, and transport phenomena.

🌱 Research Impact

Developed innovative experimental platforms including the Phytofluidic Device (POD) and Soil Matrix-on-a-Chip (SMaC) for studying plant–environment interactions.

📚 Professional Recognition

Active participant in national and international conferences, interdisciplinary collaborations, and research activities spanning plant science, engineering, and microfluidics.
