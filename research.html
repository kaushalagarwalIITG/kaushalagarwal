Research Themes

My research integrates plant biology, engineering, and microfluidics to understand how plants interact with their physical and chemical environment. I develop experimental platforms and analytical tools to study root growth, transport processes, stress responses, and sustainable agricultural technologies.

🌱 Phytofluidics

Developing plant-on-a-chip platforms to recreate realistic plant microenvironments for studying root growth, nutrient transport, and plant–environment interactions under controlled conditions.

🪴 Root Biology & Soil Microenvironment Engineering

Investigating root morphodynamics, biomechanics, thigmomorphogenesis, and root–soil interactions using Soil Matrix-on-a-Chip (SMaC) systems that mimic structured soil environments.

🌿 Plant Stress Biology

Understanding plant responses to salinity, nutrient availability, mechanical stimulation, and environmental stress through physiological, biochemical, and molecular analyses.

💧 Microfluidics & Transport Phenomena

Designing microfluidic devices and studying fluid flow, mass transport, and nutrient delivery to investigate biological processes at the microscale.

🖥️ Computational Modelling

Applying numerical modelling and simulations to analyse transport phenomena, oxygen diffusion, root mechanics, and microfluidic system performance.

🧪 Nanobiotechnology

Developing nanomaterials and nano-enabled strategies for plant protection, disease management, and sustainable crop production.

🔬 Advanced Imaging & Plant Phenotyping

Combining optical microscopy, Raman spectroscopy, fluorescence imaging, and image analysis for quantitative characterization of plant growth and physiological responses.

🌾 Sustainable Agriculture & Biosensing

Developing biosensors, paper-based analytical devices, and precision agriculture technologies for crop monitoring, plant health assessment, and agricultural sustainability.
